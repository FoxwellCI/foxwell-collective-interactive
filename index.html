Foxwell Collective Interactive is an indie studio composed of only two people. Both collaborators are responsible for concept art, writing, story development and character creation.

By choice, the members of the studio prefer not to reveal their real identities at this time. Privacy and security are absolute priorities, valued above public exposure or individual recognition.

The studio is led by Pol, publicly known as QuietPollen, who serves as the Lead Developer and primary coordinator of the project. This role is fulfilled under a separate public profile that does not contain personal or identifying information.

Current Project

The studio already has a project that is nearly complete in terms of narrative structure, universe, characters and gameplay planning.

It is a narrative-driven horror game within the Mascot Horror genre, focused on emotional immersion, direct interaction with characters and psychological tension rather than explicit gore.

The expected release window is between 2026 and 2027, allowing the necessary time for creative refinement and overall quality.

Studio Philosophy

Foxwell Collective Interactive believes that the most impactful horror does not rely solely on visual shock, but on emotional connection, quiet discomfort and the narrative consequences of the player's choices.

Each project is treated as an authorial experience, where narrative, atmosphere and characters work together to create memorable and unsettling endings.

FAQ

What is Foxwell Collective Interactive?

It is an indie studio focused on creating narrative horror games, with a strong emphasis on story, characters and psychological immersion.

How many people are part of the studio?

Only two. The studio is led by Pol (QuietPollen), with both collaborators sharing creative responsibilities across narrative and conceptual development.

Why does the studio not reveal the real identities of its members?

Because privacy and security are core values of the studio. Public-facing roles and aliases are used without exposing personal or identifying information.

What is the purpose of the studio?

To create horror experiences that respect the player, encourage reflection and leave lasting emotional impact.

Does the studio plan to work only with horror?

At present, the focus is on narrative horror. However, the studio’s creative philosophy allows for the exploration of other genres in the future, as long as they maintain narrative depth and a strong authorial identity.
