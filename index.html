Foxwell Collective Interactive

Foxwell Collective Interactive is an independent Brazilian indie game studio dedicated to the creation of narrative-driven and atmospheric interactive experiences. The studio was formed with the intention of exploring storytelling, emotional tension and immersive worlds through interactive media.

The studio operates with a strong focus on creative consistency, long-term vision and meaningful development processes rather than fast production cycles or trend-oriented design choices. Every project is approached with care, iteration and attention to detail.

Foxwell Collective Interactive is operated by two developers under the age of 18. All activities, public communication, project decisions and external interactions are supervised and supported by legal guardians to ensure compliance with applicable laws and platform requirements.

Our goal is to create experiences that feel intentional, cohesive and emotionally grounded, where atmosphere, narrative and gameplay work together rather than existing as isolated elements.

Studio Structure

The studio is publicly represented by Pol, also known as QuietPollen, who acts as the Lead Developer and primary coordinator of the project. Pol focuses on technical development, narrative structure, gameplay systems design and overall project organization.

In addition to technical responsibilities, Pol also oversees public communication, manages official studio accounts and ensures that all shared information aligns with internal guidelines and long-term creative goals.

The second core developer contributes actively to visual design, concept art and the overall aesthetic identity of the project. This includes visual storytelling, mood definition and maintaining artistic consistency across the experience.

Due to age-related platform restrictions, this developer does not maintain public or official social media accounts at this time. Despite this limitation, their creative work remains essential to the project and continues uninterrupted.

All creative contributions are valid, protected and recognized internally. Public presence is not a requirement for creative impact within the studio.

Current Project

Foxwell Collective Interactive is currently developing a narrative-focused project within the Mascot Horror genre. The project prioritizes psychological tension, environmental storytelling and emotional immersion rather than explicit or graphic content.

The project’s universe, characters and foundational concepts were developed over a long period prior to the studio’s public formation. Many ideas existed in written form long before active development began.

The narrative has undergone multiple significant revisions, including major structural changes, tone adjustments and conceptual refinements. This process lasted nearly a year and allowed the story to mature naturally.

Development is intentionally paced to allow reflection, iteration and creative stability. The estimated release window is between 2026 and 2027, ensuring sufficient time for polish and consistency.

Studio Philosophy

Foxwell Collective Interactive believes that effective horror and tension are created through atmosphere, pacing and emotional consequence rather than shock value alone.

Writing, visual design, sound and gameplay systems are developed together as interconnected components of a single experience. No element exists in isolation.

The studio values intentional design decisions, long-term identity and creative coherence over immediate visibility or short-lived trends.

Legal & Rights

Foxwell Collective Interactive and its members are protected under Brazilian law and international copyright principles. All original content is legally protected from the moment of creation.

This protection includes, but is not limited to, narratives, characters, concepts, visual assets, logos, designs, written materials and any original creative work produced by the studio.

Being under 18 years of age does not invalidate or remove intellectual property rights. Creative ownership remains legally valid and enforceable.

Legal guardians supervise contractual matters and public representation when required, ensuring compliance with applicable regulations and platform policies.

Unauthorized use, reproduction, redistribution or misrepresentation of studio content is not permitted.

Team Guidelines

The Team Guidelines function as the internal constitution of Foxwell Collective Interactive. All collaborators, contributors and representatives must comply fully with these rules.

Core Principles

• Respect creative integrity and internal trust.
• Maintain confidentiality regarding internal materials and discussions.
• Communicate responsibly and transparently.
• Protect the studio’s public image and long-term vision.

Allowed Content

• Approved development updates that do not reveal sensitive information.
• Cleared artwork, screenshots or promotional materials.
• Personal thoughts related to development when clearly marked as opinion.

Prohibited Content

• Unreleased story details or major plot points.
• Source code, internal tools or private builds.
• Private conversations or internal disagreements.
• Personal data of any individual.
• Illegal, harmful or abusive content.

Enforcement

Violations are handled proportionally depending on severity and may result in warnings, suspension or permanent removal from the project.

Curiosities & Background
